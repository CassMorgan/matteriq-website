Technical Requirements

What we need from your team to get your dashboard live — usually resolved in a single 90-minute session.

Our commitment: We do the heavy lifting. Your team's total time investment is approximately 30 minutes of access coordination. Everything else is on us. Most firms are live within 3 days of first contact.

What We're Installing (And What We're Not)

The FirmEdge dashboard is a read-only reporting layer that connects to your existing practice management database. We do not install software on your workstations, modify your existing systems, or store your client data on external servers.

Item Details Our approach
Your practice management data PC Law, Clio, MyCase, or other Read-only access only. We never write to your database.
Dashboard hosting Hosted on your local server or secure cloud Accessible from any browser on your network. No app installs.
Client data storage None Data never leaves your environment. Queries run against your DB, results display in your browser.
Workstation changes None required Staff open a URL in any browser. Nothing to install on individual machines.

What We Need From You

The checklist below covers everything required before your Launch Session. Most items take 5 minutes to locate.

  • Practice management software name and version e.g., "PC Law version 10" or "Clio Manage" — usually on the login screen or About page
  • Read-only database credentials (PC Law / local DB only) SQL Server username and password with SELECT permissions only. Your IT contact can create these in 5 minutes. We provide instructions.
  • Server IP address or hostname (PC Law / local DB only) The internal network address of the server running your practice management software. Usually something like 192.168.1.x.
  • Network access confirmation Confirmation that the machine running our dashboard can reach your database server. For most small firms this is automatic on the same network.
  • IT contact name and availability (if applicable) If you have an IT person or company, we'll coordinate directly with them to minimize your involvement. One 15-minute call is usually all they need.
  • Your firm logo (PNG or SVG) For branding your dashboard. Any version will work — we'll format it properly.

For Firms Using Cloud-Based Software (Clio, MyCase)

If your practice management software is cloud-hosted, the setup is even simpler — no server access required.

  • API access authorization A one-click authorization in your Clio or MyCase settings. Takes 2 minutes. We walk you through it on the call.
  • Admin login for the authorization step You (or your office manager) will need to be logged into your practice management software during setup.

Launch Session Timeline

Here's exactly what happens during your 90-minute launch session:

0–15 min
Demo with your firm's data structure We show you the dashboard configured for your system. You see what it will look like with your real data.
15–35 min
Access setup We connect to your database live on screen. If IT is needed, this is their 15-minute window. Most small firms: the office manager has the credentials.
35–65 min
Live dashboard with real data Your actual AR data appears on screen. We walk through every section together and answer questions.
65–90 min
You're live Dashboard is running, staff are trained, you have a browser bookmark. We handle any cleanup after the call.
Heads up for IT contacts: Our access requirements are intentionally minimal. We request read-only SQL credentials (SELECT only, no INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE permissions), access to one internal port (default 1433 for SQL Server), and no changes to your firewall or domain policy. A one-page network security summary is available on request.
