☕ What You're Building

  • A building you own in Clarkston — not renting, owning
  • Ground floor: ThirdCoast Coffee — local Michigan beans, casual, self-serve, yours
  • Upper floor: 4-6 attorney suites — leased to other attorneys, your firm anchors it
  • Three income streams — coffee profits + suite rents + your firm's rent eliminated
  • Timeline: 2-3 years from decision to open doors

📋 What Has to Happen

  • Zoning check — one phone call to Independence Township, free, takes 10 minutes
  • Property check — is Citation Drive still available? Commercial RE agent pulls this
  • SBA 504 loan — designed for owner-occupied commercial, ~10% down, your firm is the anchor
  • Architect feasibility study — confirms what you can build and what it costs ($5-15k)
  • Full design + permits — 3-6 months, $30-75k
  • Construction — 12-18 months, $400k-800k total (mostly financed)
  • Pre-lease suites — your network fills these before you open
  • Open ThirdCoast — you run the coffee shop for 6 months, then hire a manager

💡 Why This Works

  • Your firm anchors the loan — lenders love owner-occupied commercial
  • Self-serve coffee = low labor — 1-2 part-time people, not 6 full-timers
  • You know every probate attorney in Oakland County — suites pre-fill from your network
  • Clarkston is growing — no competition for this concept in the market
  • Michigan local beans = real story — "Third Coast Coffee" means something here
  • Eliminates your current rent — saves $18k-36k/year immediately

⚠️ Honest Risks

  • Two businesses is real work — hiring a coffee shop manager in month 6 is non-negotiable
  • Construction always runs over — budget a 15-20% contingency, no exceptions
  • Coffee shops can fail — self-serve model reduces this risk significantly
  • 2-3 years is a long time — the dream has to stay alive through the process

🏁 First Move

  • Call Independence Township zoning — one call, free, unlocks everything
  • Ask: "Is Citation Drive zoned for mixed-use commercial including food service?"
  • If yes: everything else can move forward
  • If no: find an alternative parcel nearby (there will be one)
